# 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy



## 中文版 (Chinese Version)

最后更新日期：2026年3月18日

本应用“我的相纸库存”（以下简称“我们”）致力于保护您的个人隐私。本政策旨在说明我们如何处理您的数据，特别是涉及相机使用、相册读写、云端同步及应用内购买的相关信息。

1\. 数据存储、iCloud 同步与交易数据

* 本地存储： 您的所有库存信息（包括相纸/胶卷名称、价格、过期时间、数量及自定义图片）默认存储在您的设备本地。
* iCloud 同步： 为了方便您在多个 iOS 设备间同步数据，本应用集成了 Apple iCloud 服务。开启此功能后，您的数据将通过 Apple 提供的加密通道上传并存储在您的个人 iCloud 账户中。我们无法访问您的 iCloud 账户，也无法读取其中的任何数据。
* 交易与订阅信息： 本应用提供应用内购买与订阅服务（Pro 会员）。所有的支付流程均由 Apple 公司通过 App Store 安全处理。我们不会收集或存储您的任何财务信息，仅接收来自 Apple 的匿名购买凭证，用于为您解锁相应的高级功能。

2\. 权限调用与具体用途 为了实现功能，我们需要调用以下权限：

* 相机权限：
  * 自定义图片：用于拍摄相纸或器材的照片，以便在库存和我的相机中展示。
  * 扫码识别：用于扫描商品的 SN 序列号或条形码，帮助您快速识别并录入商品信息。
* 相册权限（读取）： 用于从本地相册中选择并上传已有的相纸图片作为展示图。
* 相册权限（写入/仅添加）： 用于实现“导出库存图片”功能，将应用生成的库存海报直接保存至您的设备相册中。我们严格承诺，此权限仅用于存图，绝不会扫描或读取您相册中的其他无关照片。

说明： 所有通过相机拍摄或从相册选取的图片、扫描的条码信息均仅用于 App 的本地功能实现，绝不会上传至我们的服务器或任何第三方机构。

3\. 第三方服务 本应用目前不集成任何第三方广告或跟踪插件（SDK）。所有数据同步与交易验证逻辑均通过 Apple 官方接口（iCloud & StoreKit）完成。

4\. 联系方式 如有疑问，请通过以下邮箱联系我们：bo@dubobo.me

## English Version

Last Updated: March 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how "我的相纸库存" (our App) handles your data, specifically regarding camera usage, photo library access, cloud synchronization, and in-app purchases.

1\. Data Storage, iCloud Sync, and Transactions

* Local Storage: All your inventory data (including film/paper names, prices, expiration dates, quantities, and custom images) is stored on your device by default.
* iCloud Synchronization: To help you sync data across your iOS devices, the App uses Apple’s iCloud service. When enabled, your data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to your iCloud account or any data stored within it.
* Purchases and Subscriptions: The App offers in-app purchases for premium features (Pro Membership). All payments are securely processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not collect or store your financial information; we only receive anonymous receipts from Apple to verify your purchase and unlock features.

2\. Permissions and Their Usage The App requires the following permissions to provide its features:

* Camera:
  * Custom Images: Used to take photos of your film or photo equipment for visual identification within inventory management and "My Cameras."
  * Scanning: To scan SN (Serial Number) or barcodes for quick product recognition and data entry.
* Photo Library (Read): To select and upload existing images from your device for your inventory items.
* Photo Library (Add Only / Write): Used for the "Export Image" feature, allowing you to save generated inventory posters directly to your device's photo album. We strictly promise that this permission is used only for saving images and will never be used to scan or read other unrelated photos in your library.

Note: All images and scanned data are processed locally for the App's functionality and are never uploaded to our servers or any third parties.

3\. Third-Party Services The App does not integrate any third-party advertising or tracking SDKs. All synchronization and transaction verification are handled through official Apple APIs (iCloud & StoreKit).

4\. Contact Us For any questions regarding this policy, please contact us at: bo@dubobo.me
